Calve Raises

Benefits
Works Calfs

Exercise
While sitting down place your feet a hips width apart, raise up onto the balls of your feet and contract your calve muscles, hold this contraction for 8 to 10 seconds while breathing normally, relax then repeat. To up this exercise place a weight like a book or box on your lap
