Chair Pigeon

Benefits
Stretches the thighs, groins, back, and psoas. Opens the chest and shoulders.

Exercise
Sit down with your feet a shoulders width apart, bring your right leg up so your right ankle is resting on your left thigh trying to keep the right knee inline with your right ankle. Inhale while bending forward then exhale and return to your starting position, repeat on the other side.
