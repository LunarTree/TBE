Neck Strengthening 2

Benefits
Improves neck stength, and can lower voice tone over time

Exercise
Drop your head to the left, you should feel the muscles stretch on the right side of your neck, place your left hand on your head and apply a slight pressure. Now drop your chin, you may need to move your hand to apply the correct pressure, you should also feel the stretching of the neck muscles further to the back of your neck. Repeat on the right.
