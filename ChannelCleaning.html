Channel Cleaning

Exercise
Sitting down crossed legged and sitting up straight, close your right nostril with your thumb and inhale through your left nostril, then close your left nostril with your little finger and release and exhale through your right nostril, keeping the right nostril open inhale, close and exhale through your left nostril. This is one cycle, repeat this 3 to 5 times making sure you are relaxed and only thinking about your breathing
