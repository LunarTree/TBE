The Claw

Benefits
Helps strengthen wrist and hand muscles and can help relieve strain caused by tasks requiring repetitive motions.

Exercise
With your palm facing downwards hold your hand out in front of you, bend each finger so you are touching the base of each finger, your hand should look like a claw, hold this for 30 to 60 seconds and release then repeat
