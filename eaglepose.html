Eagle Pose

Benefits
Stretches the upper arms and upper back, and releases any tension in the shoulders.

Exercise
While seated lift your arms so that they are in front of you, exhale and cross your arms at the elbow so that your hands are back to back, raise your arms while crossed so you have a right angle at your elbows, hold this position for 15 to 20 seconds while breathing deeply


Eagle Pose: Advanced Version

Benefits
Stretches the upper arms and upper back, and releases any tension in the shoulders.

Exercise
Using the same technique as the Eagle Pose, but this time cross your arms so that your palms are now facing
