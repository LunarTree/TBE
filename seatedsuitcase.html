Seated Suitcase

Benefits
Works the abdominals, and thighs

Exercise
Sit on the edge of the chair seat and lean back until your upper back touches the back of the chair. Tuck your tailbone under, and hold onto the arms of the chair for support. Bring your knees up so that your shins are parallel to the floor. Your torso and thighs should resemble an open suitcase at this point. Bring your chest and knees together to close the suitcase, perform this opening and closing of the suitcase for 10 to 20 reps
