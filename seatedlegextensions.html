Seated Leg Extensions

Benefits
Works your thigh and hip muscles.

Exercise
Sitting on the edge of your chair keep your arms by your side and extend one leg out straight so your heel is on the floor, flex your foot while lifting the leg as high as possible without falling backwards. Repeat on the opposite leg doing about 10 reps on each.
