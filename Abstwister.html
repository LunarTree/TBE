Abs Twister

Benefits
Works Internal and External oblique, abdominal

Exercise
The Abs Twister will target your obliques while strengthening your core. Sitting on the edge of your chair, inhale a deep breath and contract your abdominal muscles, holding the contraction, exhale slowly squeezing your abdominals tight and turning your upper body to the left, inhale and twist your body back to your starting position, repeat on the right side doing 20 reps. To take this exercise to the next level grip a bottle of water with both hands and / or widen your leg stance while performing this exercise
