Pursed-Lip Breathing

Benefits
Pursed-Lip breathing will help you slow down your breath, opens up your lungs to allow a better exhale.

Exercise
Inhale deeply using your abdomen, pucker your lips like you were going to blow out some candles. Breathe out very slowly until your out of breath.

Advanced
Inhale through your nose like you're sniffing in for 2 seconds, pucker your lips like you were going to blow out some candles. Breathe out very slowly, your exhale should last double the length of your inhale. Repeat.
