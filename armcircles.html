Arm Circles

Benefits
Works the arms and upper back but also targets the shoulders and chest.

Exercise
Hold your arms out straight to your side with your palms down, press your shoulder blades together, slowly rotate your arms in a circular motion do 20 reps in each direction then flip your hands so your palms are facing up and repeat the process
