Oblique Reaches

Benefits
Works your oblique muscles and abdominals

Exercise
Extend out your legs and place your hands behind head keeping your elbows parallel to your head and contract your abdominals. Twist to the right so your elbow almost touches your right leg, twist back to the starting position and repeat on the other side doing this for about 20 reps
