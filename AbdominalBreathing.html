Abdominal Breathing

Exercise
Start by sitting comfortably on the floor, place one hand on your stomach and the other on your back. Breathe out and empty yourself of air, while taking deep breaths through your nose feel both hands move away from your spine. Continue to do this for 5 breaths then relax and continue at your own pace.
