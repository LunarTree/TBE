Extended Triceps Press

Benefits
Works the front of your shoulders and triceps

Exercise
Sitting straight with your abdominals squeezed hold a full bottle of water in your left hand, with your arm fully extended in front of you, pause then continue lifting the bottle all the way over your head, when your arm touches your ear bend the elbow and contract your triceps, continue moving your arm over your head.
