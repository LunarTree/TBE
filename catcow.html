Chair Cat / Cow Pose

Benefits
Improves spinal flexibility and abdominal strength.

Exercise
Sit down with your feet a shoulders width apart, keep your hands in a relaxed position on your thighs. While inhaling sit up tall, arch your back, and drop and push your shoulders backwards. Hold this position for 10 seconds, on the exhale arch forward your spine allowing your shoulders and head to drop forward, hold for 10 seconds and return to the starting position and repeat.
