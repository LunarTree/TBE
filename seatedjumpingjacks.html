Seated Jumping Jacks

Benefits
Good cardio, gets the heart rate up and blood pumping

Exercise
While sitting on the edge of your chair, extend your arms and legs as if you were doing a normal jumping jack, then return them to a closed position with your elbows and hands together in front of your chest and your legs close. Repeat this about 20 reps and increasing reps and speed as you feel more comfortable
